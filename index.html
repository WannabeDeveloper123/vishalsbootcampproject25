Summer Trip Back Home

Singapore, if you haven't known, is a tiny island country located on the peninsular tip of Malaysia. In fact, Singapore is so small that you'd have to look at the world map through a microscope to find where it's located. It's a country where you could get from one end to the other while watching a Coldplay concert (yikes!)... unless you end up behind a truck doing 40 on the passing lane. Putting my witty metaphors aside, I'll admit, it does get pretty hot out there. Anyway, here's a pic of the famous Marina Bay Sands Hotel, which I've taken about a thousand times considering Singapore's my home for the past 12 years.
